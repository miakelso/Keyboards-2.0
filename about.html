About Forge

Forge builds mechanical keyboards for people who want performance, personality, and long-term quality. From premium pre-built boards to fully custom setups, our goal is simple: make every keystroke feel better.

Our Mission

Deliver reliable, high-performance keyboards with modern design and customization options that fit each user.

What We Stand For

  • Quality materials and dependable builds
  • Transparent pricing and clear product details
  • Fast, friendly support for every customer

Why Forge

Every keyboard is selected or assembled with care so gamers, creators, and professionals can type with confidence.

Our Story

Forge started with a small team of keyboard enthusiasts who wanted better options than generic office hardware. We believed your keyboard should be more than a tool — it should match your style and improve your workflow.

Today, we continue to grow by listening to our customers and improving every generation of our boards, switches, and build options.
